History

The history of New England is an incredibly interesting topic. New England was founded by English colonists who killed literally everybody they crossed paths with. Thus, the region could be thought of as a mass-indian grave spanning thousands of square miles, and a testament to the unbridled cruelty of humanity brought on by greed.
