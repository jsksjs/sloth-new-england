The Beach Plum

One of two places in New England where you can dab on a statue of a giant anthropomorphic lobster in a parking lot!

The Beach Plum is very cool. They have, like, 100 flavors of ice cream, and like, one flavor of lobster roll. They're officially endorsed by the National United States High School AP Chemistry Teacher's Culinary Recommendation Association of America (NUSHSAPCTCRAoA), so you know they're good.
