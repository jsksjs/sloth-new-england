Misquamicut

Misquamicut

Misquamicut is one of the last vestiges of the ancestral New England Sand Lands. Highway development has siphoned most of the area's sand off into the sea for the purpose of raising the sea level in the hopes that annual flooding would wash highways for free, but sadly this has only led to the extinction of native sand populations.
