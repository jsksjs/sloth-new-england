University of Hartford

University of Hartford, also known as UHart, is located in West Hartford, Connecticut. UHart is a private university and is home to students from 48 states and 43 countries. A variety of academic programs are offered at UHart. There are seven distint schools and colleges which includes the College of Arts & Sciences, the Barney School of Business, the College of Education, Nursing, and Health Professions, the College of Engineering, Technology, and Architecture, the Hartt School, the Hartford Art School, and Hillyer College. Altogether, there are 84 bachelor's degree programs, 11 associate's degrees, 38 graduate degrees, and seven certificates or diplomas offered.
