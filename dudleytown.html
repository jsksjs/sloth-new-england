Dudleytown

Dudleytown Village Entrance

Dudleytown resides in Cornwall, Connecticut and was founded in the mid-1740s. It was abandoned in the 1800s and remains inhabitn't. Today, it is known as a ghost town and has been labeled as the "Village of the Damned."

There have been many mysterious events such as suicide, fatal accidents, vanishings, and unexplained sightings that have led to a number of articles in the media. All of this attention has led to many investigators, journalists, hikers, and curious visitors. However, trespassing on Dudleytown property is strictly forbidden.
