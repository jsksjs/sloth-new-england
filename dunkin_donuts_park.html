Dunkin' Donuts Stadium

The Dunkin' Donuts Stadium is a baseball park located in Hartford, Connecticut. It has 6,121 seats and can hold up to 6,850 people. The stadium is the home field of the Hartford Yard Goats of the Eastern League.
