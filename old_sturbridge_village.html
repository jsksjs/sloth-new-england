Old Sturbridge Village

Old Sturbridge Village


Old Sturbridge Village is an enigma. This place does its best to recreate an arbitrarily old version of Connecticut, just as it was before now. In fact, it's so old, probably a lot of people don't have rights and it isn't a good place to live.

Is everybody there crazy? Or are they just really, really, old?

Only you can be the judge.
