What Is New England?

In the northeast corner of the United States lies New England, home to some of the nation's most popular cultural attractions, parks, universities, and sports teams. A good travel destination for anyone due to its four seasons and variety of cities and towns. New England is made up of six states: Connecticut, Maine, Massachusetts, New Hampshire, Rhode Island, and Vermont.
